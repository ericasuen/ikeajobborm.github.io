Kitchen Production Manager

Richmond, BC


Food & Restaurant


Travel Requirements: 0 - 10%


Type of Job: Full Time

Job Description

What you'll need to have

  • have 3 years of work experience in managing a high-volume restaurant where you took care of food production, ordering, inventory, operations, budget, staffing, and team development
  • have at least 3 years of proven formal Leadership experience
  • have ideas for, and experience implementing, food-waste-reduction programs and are always looking for more sustainable processes
  • have amazing attention for details and can effortlessly juggle multiple competing priorities while keeping a smile on your face
  • are a total people-person and can strike up a conversation easily
  • have an obvious passion for IKEA’s food offering, products and design, as well as our Sustainability commitment
  • Your management style can only be summed up in like this: open door, empowering and supportive

What you'll be doing day to day

  • Be an IKEA Ambassador by understanding that you impact our customer’s experience by being able to offer solutions and answer questions that relate to their kitchen and home furnishing needs
  • Coach, manage, mentor and on-board new and existing team members to ensure that they succeed. This includes identifying and developing internal talent for the future
  • Responsible for ensuring Global and National compliance standards and processes are implemented and followed, and that the team maintains a safety-first mindset
  • Maintain lean operations and inventory, concentrating on reducing food waste
  • Ensuring compost/food waste programs are carried out responsibly
  • Ensure that kitchen is always in optimal state, and that equipment is well maintained and always in working order
  • Partner with the Commercial Restaurant Manager to further Business objectives
  • Create healthy and delicious meals for the co-worker restaurant
  • Support and contribute to the IKEA Retail Food Business plan and Sustainability Business plan
  • Stay up to date with our product line and be prepared to educate both customers and co-workers on their ingredients, allergens, and availability.
