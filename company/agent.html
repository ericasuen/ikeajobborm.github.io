Safety and Security Agent

Richmond, BC


Risk & Compliance


Travel Requirements: 0 - 10%


Type of Job: Full Time

Job Description

What you'll need to have

  • have at least one year of experience in the field of loss prevention or a similar field.
  • hold a valid Ontario Security Guard license, you are a Certified First Aid and CPR Responder.
  • pay great attention to detail, are incredibly approachable and have a strong sense of responsibility.
  • accurate and timely when conducting your investigations and reports.
  • keep your cool under pressure and can react quickly and confidently in a crisis.
  • manage sensitive and confidential information with extreme professionalism and integrity.
  • have no problem working in a fast-paced (and sometimes stressful) environment.
  • reliability and knowledge make you a resource person for our co-workers who need training and support on health, safety, and security issues
  • have a basic knowledge of local and federal laws and court procedures.
  • Previous Loss Prevention / floor walking experience is an asset

What you'll be doing day to day

  • Ensure that the environment is always safe and secure in and on our property, for our customers and coworker.
  • Respect and apply all appropriate IKEA health and safety procedures and ensure that all other co-workers do the same.
  • Respond promptly to all health and safety needs and perform first aid or other emergency/safety procedures necessary to meet those needs.
  • Open and close the store in accordance with IKEA procedures.
  • Regularly conducts all operational and security audits, as required.
  • Conducts assigned health and safety investigations and follows up on all incident reports in a timely manner.
  • Takes responsibility for other tasks and projects, including helping your co-workers carry out their responsibilities when necessary.
  • Actively promote theft prevention awareness across the unit and support with educating coworkers into a security mindset
  • Conduct lawful apprehensions for theft & fraud and other criminal activity.
  • Take a proactive approach with initiating and completing investigations on internal and external matters in accordance with legal and INGKA requirements.
