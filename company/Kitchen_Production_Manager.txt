		<h2>Kitchen Production Manager</h2>

	<i class="fas fa-map-marker-alt"></i><h3>Richmond, BC</h3>
	<br>
	<i class="fa-regular fa-list"></i> <h3>Food &amp; Restaurant</h3>
  <br>
  <i class="fa-regular fa-plane"></i><h3>Travel Requirements: 0 - 10%</h3>
  <br>
    <h3> Type of Job: Full Time</h3>
		<hr>
<p>Job Description</p>
<p>What you'll need to have </p>
<ul>
<li> have 3 years of work experience in managing a high-volume restaurant where you took care of food production, ordering, inventory, operations, budget, staffing, and team development </li>
<li> have at least 3 years of proven formal Leadership experience</li>
<li> have ideas for, and experience implementing, food-waste-reduction programs and are always looking for more sustainable processes </li>
<li> have amazing attention for details and can effortlessly juggle multiple competing priorities while keeping a smile on your face</li>
<li> are a total people-person and can strike up a conversation easily</li>
<li> have an obvious passion for IKEA’s food offering, products and design, as well as our Sustainability commitment</li>
<li>Your management style can only be summed up in like this: open door, empowering and supportive</li>
</ul>

<p>What you'll be doing day to day</p>
<ul>
<li>Be an IKEA Ambassador by understanding that you impact our customer’s experience by being able to offer solutions and answer questions that relate to their kitchen and home furnishing needs</li>
<li>Coach, manage, mentor and on-board new and existing team members to ensure that they succeed. This includes identifying and developing internal talent for the future</li>
<li>Responsible for ensuring Global and National compliance standards and processes are implemented and followed, and that the team maintains a safety-first mindset </li>
<li>Maintain lean operations and inventory, concentrating on reducing food waste</li>
<li>Ensuring compost/food waste programs are carried out responsibly </li>
<li>Ensure that kitchen is always in optimal state, and that equipment is well maintained and always in working order</li>
<li>Partner with the Commercial Restaurant Manager to further Business objectives</li>
<li> Create healthy and delicious meals for the co-worker restaurant</li>
<li>Support and contribute to the IKEA Retail Food Business plan and Sustainability Business plan</li>
<li>Stay up to date with our product line and be prepared to educate both customers and co-workers on their ingredients, allergens, and availability.</li>

</ul>
