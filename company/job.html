Kitchens, Dining & Appliances Shopkeeper II

Richmond, BC


Sales & Commercial


Travel Requirements: 0 - 10%

Type of Job: Full Time

Job Description

What you'll need to have

  • You have a genuine passion for home furnishings and the ability to quickly develop in-depth knowledge of the local market.
  • You have the aptitude to collaborate across functions, as well as to influence and communicate effectively at all levels of a fast-paced, change oriented organization.
  • You have 3-5 years of retail management experience and/or an undergraduate degree in Retail Management.
  • You have a dynamic and entrepreneurial leadership profile with a “hands-on” approach.
  • You have a well-developed strategic, organizational and decision-making skills.
  • You have proven P&L accountability and advanced computer literacy.

What you'll be doing day to day

  • Direct responsibility for the management of one or more Home Furnishing Businesses within our store.
  • Ensure that the department of your responsibility operates efficiently and profitably providing our customers with a distinctive and delightful shopping experience in line with our concept.
  • Develop annual action plan to execute business plans, meet or exceed sales goals and related P&L objectives.
  • Optimize sales, customer satisfaction and a sustainable profitability through range presentation.
  • Lead the continuous development of your sales department ensuring customer service standards are met or exceeded.
  • Actively contribute to the sales management team and to the building of business competence by supporting and coaching co-workers through knowledge, confidence, trust and motivation so they are always ready to assist customers in the best way possible.
  • Undertake Duty Manager and other storewide managerial responsibilities as required.
